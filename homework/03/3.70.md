# 3.70

## A

根据联合的定义

|val|offset|
|---|------|
|e1.p|0|
|e1.y|8|
|e2.x|0|
|e2.next|8|

## B

sizeof(ele) = max(sizeof(ele.e1), sizeof(ele.e2)) = 16

## C

```text
void proc (union ele *up)
up in %rsi
```

执行 `movq 8(%rdi), %rax`

```text
     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- %rax
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdi (up)

  此时，
    %rax = up->e1.y
  或
    %rax = up->e2.next
```

执行 `movq (%rax), %rdx`

```text
    (%rax) 表示，此时把 %rax 存储的值当做内存地址，所以
    %rax = up->e2.next

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- %rax
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdi (up)

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdx (up->e2.next)

  此时，
    %rdx = up->e2.next->e1.p
  或
    %rdx = up->e2.next->e2.x
```

执行 `movq (%rdx), %rdx`

```text
    (%rdx) 表示，此时把 %rdx 存储的值当做内存地址，所以
    %rdx = up->e2.next->e1.p

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- %rax
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdi (up)

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- up->e2.next
  
  %rdx = *(up->e2.next->e1.p)
```

执行 `subq 8(%rax), %rdx`

```text
    8(%rax) 表示，此时把 %rax 存储的值当做内存地址，所以
    %rax = up->e2.next

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- %rax
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdi (up)

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- 8(%rax)
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- up->e2.next
  
  subq 意味着 8(%rax) 表示整数，所以
  8(%rax) = up->e2.next->eq.y
  最后，
  %rbx = *(up->e2.next->e1.p) - up->e2.next->eq.y
```

执行 `movq %rdx, (%rdi)`

```text

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- %rax
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- %rdi (up)

     +-------------------+
     | e1.y  or  e2.next |
  8  +-------------------+ <-- 8(%rax)
     | e1.p  or  e2.x    |
  0  +-------------------+ <-- up->e2.next

  %rbx = *(up->e2.next->e1.p) - up->e2.next->eq.y
  (%rdi) 用来存放 %rdx 中保存的整型数据
  (%rai) = up->e2.x = *(up->e2.next->e1.p) - up->e2.next->eq.y
``
